About

Dr. M. Babu is a Professor and Director i/c at Bharathidasan School of Management, Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli. With over 15 years of teaching experience and 21 years in research, his primary areas of specialization include Management, Financial Markets and Services, and Behavioral Finance.
